WAFFLES

A beautiful waffle recipe by "LAND O' LAKES"

This recipe is made by "LAND O' LAKES" a brand that manufactures butter and other dairy and non-dairy products. Their recipe includes the typical ingredients that you would see in a waffle recipe. Things like: butter, eggs, milk, flour sugar, and salt. Also, according to this article, you can freeze any waffles that you don't eat! They dont go into any details on the best way to reheat these as far as oven time and temperature, but you're a smart cookie, im sure you can figure it out ;)

This recipe will take an approximate time of 10 minutes to prep, with a total time of 30 minutes for a finished product. it makes as many as "8 waffles" but depending on the size of your waffle maker, this number may vary. Below this you will see the recipe for these amazing waffles. But, before you dive in and show us your culinary prowess, remember that making waffles is meant to be fun. So regardless of the outcome, be proud of your awesome waffles. p.s. dont forget to add syrup :).

"Ingredients"

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup Land O Lakes® Butter, melted
  • 3 large Land O Lakes® Eggs, slightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

TOPPINGS

  • Land O Lakes® Butter with Canola Oil
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in bowl; mix well.
  2. Combine milk, melted butter, eggs and vanilla in another bowl; mix well. Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients.
  3. Cook batter in Belgian waffle maker according to manufacturer’s directions. Serve warm with Butter with Canola Oil and maple syrup.
