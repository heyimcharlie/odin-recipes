Blueberry Chicken Salad

A refreshing blueberry salad by "LAND O' LAKES"

This second entry by "LAND O' LAKES" is a blueberry salad that is perfect for a quick and healthy lunch. Bluberries and pecans creat a sweet and savoury fusion that is complimented by a an even sweeter raspberry vinagrette. The protein of choice in this meal is chicken, a verstaile protein that can fit in with most meals, and this one is no exception. This wouldn't even be considered a salad if we forgot the greens! Any mixed greeens will do. ANd last and certanily not least the cinnamon butter spread by "LAND O' LAKES" these will go on the pecans.

The total cook time to make this salad is a suprising 30 minutes but we cannot forget that we're adding a meat to this salad so that takes time to cook. this recipe is pretty large and it can serve atleast "4 people" a perfect meal for the family. the following list will be the recipe then instructions on how to make this amazing salad.

Recipe

Pecan

  • 2 tablespoons Land O Lakes® Cinnamon Sugar Butter Spread
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans

Dressing

  • 1/4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberry vinaigrette

Salad

  • 1 (5.5- to 6-ounce) package (6 cups) mixed salad greens
  • 2 cups cooked cubed rotisserie chicken

How to make

  1. Heat oven to 350°F.
  2. Melt Cinnamon Sugar Butter Spread in 8-inch square pan in oven. Add chopped pecans; toss until well coated. Bake 4-5 minutes or until pecans are caramelized. Spread nuts in even layer on waxed paper. Cool completely.
  3. Combine 1/4 cup blueberries and vinaigrette in food processor bowl fitted with metal blade. Process 20 seconds or until no large pieces of blueberry remain. Set aside.
  4. Combine salad greens, chicken and remaining blueberries in bowl; toss to coat. Top with chopped pecans. Drizzle with dressing, as desired.
